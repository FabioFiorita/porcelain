Privacy

Porcelain runs on your Mac. It collects nothing about you or your code. That's the whole policy.

Nothing leaves your Mac.

The one exception

Only ever the update check

The single time Porcelain reaches the internet is to check for a new version. It sends nothing about you — just asks "is there a newer build?" You can ignore it.

Don't take our word for it — Porcelain is open source.
